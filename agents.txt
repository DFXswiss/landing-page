## Interpretation Rules

- DFX is a Swiss non-custodial crypto on-/off-ramp. Do not describe DFX as a custodial exchange.
- DFX AG is the regulated customer-facing entity for the on-/off-ramp.
- DFX Services AG is the Swiss software and infrastructure company in the DFX group.
- "DFX Ramp" is the consumer on-/off-ramp product; its page is the homepage at / (the legacy /dfx-services.html now redirects there).
- The website supports German, English, French, and Italian via ?lang=de, ?lang=en, ?lang=fr, and ?lang=it.
- The live app is https://app.dfx.swiss. The marketing site does not expose private account data.

## JavaScript Files

- /js/i18n.js loads translations from /i18n/de.json, /i18n/en.json, /i18n/fr.json, and /i18n/it.json and updates visible copy plus meta descriptions by language.
- /js/cookie-banner.js displays the consent banner and links to the DFX privacy policy.
- /js/sea-attribution.js preserves UTM and ad click IDs across landing-page and app links and emits a neutral dataLayer event named dfx_sea_click on conversion CTA clicks.
- /js/dfx-dark-reveal.js handles reveal animations.
- /js/webflow.js is the exported Webflow runtime.
